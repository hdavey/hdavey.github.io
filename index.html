ABOUT ME








Welcome to my portfolio!

Currently I am in my third and final year of the Interactive Media Development program at Confederation College in Thunder Bay Ontario, Canada. Throughout the program I have focused on such things as web design, 3D graphics, graphic design, and animation, to name a few.

Outside of school, I enjoy photography, art, and travelling, and the company of my dog.
